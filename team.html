Meet The Founders



As a team of passionate college freshmen from Rutgers-New Brunswick (RU), Isha, Meethi, Rhea, and Kaajal, we are self-driven to pursue computer science. With our website, we hope that college students, like us, will become educated on their financial spendings while attending university.

We strive to provide resources for students to utilize when navigating through their financial expenses. RUMoneyWize is making a difference one step at a time.
